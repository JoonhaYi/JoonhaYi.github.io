Pulsar Science Collaboratory

Currently part of the Pulsar Science Collaboratory, analyzing radio telescope data to find new pulsars. Has analyzed over 3000 prepfold plots. Also meets with the UW Bothell PSC team every week, presenting a part of a scientific paper.

Pulsar Scintillation

The PSC Scintillation group is a subgroup within the PSC that studies the periodic change in the pulsar's brightness. My role is to process the data using Python. For example, removing interference, generating informative plots, etc.

Independent Research
